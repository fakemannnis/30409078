My Projects

Mobile Banking App

I designed a mobile banking app that allows users to easily manage their finances on-the-go. My design process included conducting user research, creating user personas, and developing user stories. I also created wireframes and prototypes, and conducted usability testing to ensure that the design was user-friendly and efficient.

E-commerce Website

I designed an e-commerce website for a local bookstore that wanted to expand their online presence. My design process included creating user personas, developing user stories, and conducting competitive analysis to identify best practices in the e-commerce industry. I also created wireframes and prototypes, and conducted usability testing to ensure that the design was accessible and efficient for users.

Healthcare Portal

I designed a healthcare portal for a hospital that wanted to improve patient engagement and streamline their services. My design process included conducting user research, creating user personas, and developing user stories. I also created wireframes and prototypes, and conducted usability testing to ensure that the design was user-friendly and accessible for patients of all ages.
